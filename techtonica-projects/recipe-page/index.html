The Most Delicious Sweet Potato & Kale Curry Recipe!

This is a super easy and healthy, basic curry recipe!! Takes less than 30 minutes, and perfect for the slow cooker too. Serve as is, or over some steamed basmati for a filling dinner that's sure to please the whole family!

  • 2 Large Sweet Potatoes chopped.
  • 3 cups of roughly chopped Kale.
  • 2 tsps minced ginger.
  • 2 tsps minced garlic.
  • 1 yellow onion(chopped).
  • 2 Tbsp Sweet or Madras curry powder.
  • 1/8 tsp hing(asafoetida)(optional) click here for usage instructions.
  • 1 tsp ground coriander.
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper.
  • 1/4 tsp cumin.
  • 1 tsp chicken or vegetable boullion.
  • Sea Salt, to taste.
